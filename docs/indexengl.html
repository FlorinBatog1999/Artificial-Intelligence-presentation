Artificial Inteligence

What is AI?

In simple terms, artificial intelligence (AI) refers to systems or machines that mimic human intelligence to perform various activities and can be improved iteratively based on the information it collects. AI manifests itself in a number of forms.

How did it come about?

The beginnings of artificial intelligence can be seen immediately after World War II, in the first programs that solved puzzles or played certain games. There were two reasons (in addition to the fascination it exerts on many) that games were among the first areas of application of artificial intelligence - first, that program performance is easy to measure (most of the time, you either win or lose a game); then, that the rules are generally simple and few in number, so they can be easily described and used. The games he usually experimented with were chess and checkers. The idea was very simple - given a position on the board, they tried to generate all possible sequences of moves from that moment on, considering that the opponent always chooses the best move. If a sequence reached a winning state, then it was the one to follow. The practical problem with this idea was that the number of combinations to be explored was very high (for example, in chess, on the order of $ 35 ^ {100} $).

How can I learn?

Python is in the top three programming languages ​​used by programmers. Due to the complexity and multitude of libraries available, Python is considered a programming language that fits the needs of the AI ​​field.

Since Python has many libraries that involve mathematical formulas, including probabilities and statistics, it would be ideal if you start learning the basics in this area of ​​mathematics.

In order to master the probability part, it is important to like mathematics in order to be able to work with dispersions and the relations between favorable and possible cases.

In order to create something useful, both in AI and in other fields, you must be persevering, optimistic and like what you do. Without these ingredients you will not get too far.
